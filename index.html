Built Different

Not another subscription app.

Film productions are project-based. Your camera log app should be too.

CamLog was built by a camera assistant tired of paying monthly subscription fees for a single-day commercial or on downtime between productions.

Other camera log apps are priced for one user on a monthly subscription. If your whole department wants access, that's a monthly bill multiplied by every team member that needs it.

CamLog has no per-user pricing and no paywalled features for single-user projects. It's a web app that opens on any phone or laptop the moment you share the link.

When you need the whole team included in real time, upgrade to CamLog Pro. Only the project admin pays.
